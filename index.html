Vision

Our team's vision is to make texts accessible by everyone by revolutionizing the way we interact with text. Motivated by the inaccessibile prose of modern day academic, industrial, and judicial texts, Noted serves as a personalized butler for readers by serving supplemental information that ensure their understanding of their texts when and where they need it.

Technology

  • Myo Gesture Control Armband

    The Myo Gesture Control Armband is integrated to remove our users from the distractions a keyboard and mouse allows.

  • Eye Tribe Tracker

    The Eye Tribe Tracker is integrated to precisely track a user's eye movements so that Noted can deduce when it should supply a user information.

  • Laptop

    Noted runs on macOS.
